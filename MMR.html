Dr. Muhammad Muhitur Rahman

Research interests

  • Wastewater treatment including membrane bioreactor
  • Effluent management and reuse in agriculture
  • Soil-plant-atmosphere interaction modelling
  • Contaminant fate and transport in vadose zone
  • Water quality monitoring
  • Solid waste management
  • Risk assessment and probabilistic modelling including Bayesian Belief Network

Biography

Dr. Muhit is a highly skilled and accomplished Water and Environmental Engineering professional with significant expertise in research, teaching, and scientific project management. He has a demonstrated ability to solve complex problems, analyze large datasets, and communicate scientific findings effectively. His work has been recognized through prestigious awards, including the Top Research Publication Award 2015 from Western Sydney University and a grant from CSIRO for investigating recycled water reuse schemes using Bayesian Networks. With a Ph.D. in Environmental Engineering from Western Sydney University and additional degrees in Civil Engineering from KFUPM and BUET, Dr. Muhit has authored over 60 scholarly publications and has a notable Google Scholar h-index of 13. His academic journey is marked by multiple scholarships, including the Australian Postgraduate Award, reflecting his dedication and excellence in the field. His contributions continue to shape sustainable water management practices.
