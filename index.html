Welcome!

Hi, my name is Bijoy Mitra. I have completed my undergraduate degree in Geography and Environmental Studies from the University of Chittagong, Bangladesh. I am actively involved in academic projects and research collaborations. Please feel free to reach out to me for academic or professional inquiries!

Research Interests

My research goals are to promote sustainable and climate change resilient strategies, focusing on overcoming climatic challenges. Some key areas of interest:

  • Climate Change and Resiliance
  • GIS & Geospatial Analysis
  • Remote Sensing
  • Real-time Air Quality Monitoring
  • Landuse and Landcover Monitoring
  • Environmental data analysis using machine learning

Bijoy Mitra has extensively studied the impact of climate change-induced sea level rise on coastal demography, focusing on the socioeconomic consequences for vulnerable populations. His research includes the development of predictive models for forest-water canopy dynamics and marine algal blooms using advanced machine-learning techniques. He has applied remote sensing technologies to analyze the spatial distribution of urban and oceanic airborne pollution, identifying pollution hotspots and their potential health risks.
